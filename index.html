Melbourne Surveillance Track

Stop 0 - Intro

Let’s set the scene for the tour. Start here!

Stop 1 - Federation Square

Let’s talk about public and private spaces, and why it’s equally important to protect our rights online as well as offline.

Stop 2 - Flinders Lane

At this intersection we will talk about the types of cameras around us as we move through cities. CCTV is everywhere, but what does it do?

Stop 3 - Burke St

Our cities and their infrastructure are often shaped by tragic events. Let’s pause here and reflect on how Melbourne was changed a couple of years ago and what infrastructure lingers on, existing as a permanent mark.

Stop 4 - QV Street Mall

Surveillance for commercial purposes is becoming increasingly commonplace. Are you aware how much data is being gathered about you and your habits as you stroll through a shopping mall?

Stop 5 - Library

Libraries are the archives of our societies. Let’s take the opportunity to talk about human rights and how we can actively shape what is considered right and what is considered lawful in our cities.
